Description

The main goal of this project is to develop a system with a view to help in the classification and recognition of Handwriting characters and digits and which will going to help the kids of the society for improving their basic writing skills for every alphabet and which is A to Z and as well as the digit which is from 0 to 9. In this we also provide a text extraction part in which the kids can upload there required pictures and the OCR system will extract the text written in the given picture and provide them the output. They can download the written text as a .txt file and take it for further use as well. The system will also provide some funny activities like they can watch some videos related to their interest to increase their knowledge as well. And the kids can also play some games for the entertainment purpose. This system will help the kids to get learn while performing different tasks.
